Policy on Expanded Access (EA) to Investigational Drugs

Currently, participation in clinical trials is the only way for patients to gain access to Generate Biomedicines’ investigational therapies. As more clinical data on the safety and efficacy of these investigational therapies become available, we will review and update our policy on Expanded Access.
